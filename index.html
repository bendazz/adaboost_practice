Front-end only · No frameworks

Exercise: Train a decision stump

  1. Download the dataset as CSV.
  2. In Python, train a decision stump on this dataset using scikit-learn (DecisionTreeClassifier with max_depth=1 and random_state=1).
  3. Compute the model's training error on the dataset.
  4. From that error, determine the decision stump's say.
Tip: Labels are generated from a linear boundary on features 1 and 2 with slight noise; feature 3 (when present) is extra.
